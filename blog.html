Adopted!

This photo is special because I found this spider on some leaves one day and many days after, I've seen it again and again. I watched this spider grow and gain more color and hairs on its legs. Like it's going through puberty *sniff*. It's also special because I actually have arachnophobia, so feelin this way toward a spider is unexpected, weird, and a blessing.

Bug identified!

Since getting into photography with my macro lens, I've taken to walking around greenery and shrubbery, looking for critters. And it's fun! Which is especially good because I'm not a bug person. But while I like looking for and finding all these little bugs, I like to also know what I've had the pleasure of capturing. This one, I couldn't identify for a while, but I recently I saw a photo of the same kind of bug (same color too!). It's called a Cotton Stainer Bug!
